The Screenshot Approach

Step 1: Find a UI you like
Step 2: Screenshot it
Step 3: Paste into Claude: "Build this"
Step 4: Get a working page in 30 seconds

It's shockingly good. For a solo dev, this is unbeatable.

But what happens when you need consistency across 10 pages?

Talking Points (1 min)

Be honest — this approach works. I've done it. You've done it. The output is 80% there. But it breaks down when you need a design SYSTEM, not just a design.
