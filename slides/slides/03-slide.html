But What About Teams?

  • Screenshots don't have tokens — every dev picks different colors
  • No change tracking — "did the designer update this?"
  • No sync — design and code drift apart immediately
  • No versioning — which screenshot is current?
  • No shared components — the footer is copy-pasted 5 times

You need a design system, not a screenshot.

Talking Points (1 min)

The fundamental problem: screenshots capture appearance but not structure. No source of truth for colors, no way to detect changes, no mechanism for sync.
