/stitch-variants <screen> WRITES TO STITCH

Generate layout, color, or font variants. Args: screen title, aspect (layout/color), creative range (explore/reimagine), count (1-5).

Undo: tombstone unwanted screen IDs

/stitch-analyse <screen> <template> READ-ONLY

Compare Stitch HTML against your template. Section mapping table — what maps, what's new, what differs. Porting checklist.

No side effects — analysis only
