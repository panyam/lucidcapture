Key moment (1 min)

"Stitch has no version history, no branching, no rollback. So we built our own — with tools engineers already trust: git, JSON files, shell scripts."

"This is the meta-lesson: when a design tool doesn't have the governance features you need, build them in your layer. Don't wait for the vendor. The sync script, the tombstone file, the structural extractor — together they ARE the version history."
