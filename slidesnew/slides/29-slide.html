Key Takeaways

  • MCP is the game-changer — not the AI generation itself, but making design tools programmable
  • Build the tooling layer — sync scripts, structural extraction, tombstoning. Stitch alone isn't enough
  • Design tokens are the real value — they drive themes, enable dark mode for free, keep stacks in sync
  • Stitch is early — no components, no themes, edit=copy. But the direction is right
  • Tool-agnostic — MCP works with Cursor, Claude Code, Windsurf, Cline. The protocol matters, not the client
